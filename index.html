End of Term One 2026 Notice

ST. ANDREWS GRAMMAR SCHOOL

Official Communication to All Parents & Guardians · April 6, 2026

Dear Parents and Guardians,

We hope this message finds you well. As we conclude the first term of the 2026 academic year, we wish to share important updates and plans for the coming term.

1. End of Term One Examinations

We are proud of the academic progress our learners have made this term. End of Term One exams will commence shortly. We encourage parents to ensure your child reviews their notes and is physically and emotionally prepared for assessment.

2. School Fees Balance Reminder

To ensure the smooth running of school operations, kindly clear any outstanding school fees balance before examinations begin. Contact our bursar's office if you require assistance with payment arrangements.

3. Term Two Educational Tour

We are pleased to announce an exciting educational tour for all students in Term Two. The tour fee is set at Ugx 50,000 per pupil. Payment can be made to the bursar's office or via school accounts at the start of Term Two.

4. Looking Ahead

We appreciate your unwavering trust and partnership in educating your children. We remain committed to providing quality education and will continue updating you on school developments.

Wishing you and your family a safe and peaceful remainder of the 2026 year.

Yours Sincerely,

The Headteacher
